About me

I'm a passionate Machine Learning and Data Science professional with over eight years of experience across diverse industries and academia. I hold an MSc. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from Rice University, a specialization in Statistics, and a degree in Electrical Engineering, all of which provide me with a strong foundation in AI research, statistical modeling, and signal processing.

My career spans roles in both research and industry, where I've developed machine learning models for various applications, including predictive analytics, time series forecasting, and computer vision. I'm passionate about applying cutting-edge algorithms to solve complex real-world problems. My ideal workplace is one in which problem-solving requires deep, creative thinking rather than following mechanical recipes.

I'm proficient in Python (including deep learning frameworks such as PyTorch and TensorFlow) and other tools like R, SQL, Docker, and cloud platforms. I love to work in collaborative environments where I can leverage my skills to bridge the gap between research and practical implementation.

Let's connect if you're interested in AI, data science, or discussing innovative solutions to challenging problems!
